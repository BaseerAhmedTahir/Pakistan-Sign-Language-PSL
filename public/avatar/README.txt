Drop your rigged avatar here as `avatar.vrm`.

Recommended: a VRM avatar made in VRoid Studio (free) or downloaded from
VRoid Hub. VRM is a standardized humanoid format with full finger bones and
native facial expressions/blendshapes (the hook for PSL non-manual grammar).

Until this file exists, the app renders a procedurally rigged placeholder
mannequin whose bone names follow the VRM humanoid spec.

Note: Ready Player Me (the previous plan) was acquired by Netflix and shut
down on 2026-01-31 — do not build on RPM exports.
